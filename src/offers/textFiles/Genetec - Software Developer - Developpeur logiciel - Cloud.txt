The team:

Genetec’s Clearance team is at the center of a revolution in digital evidence management. We’re a small, close-knit, diverse group of passionate, innovative developers and testers who build and maintain one of Genetec’s fastest growing products.

At our core, we’re about providing a safe, secure, reliable means for people to share digital evidence and help protect the everyday. To do that we work daily with concepts like software security, cloud-scale distributed systems and DevOps. We also care about diversity and inclusion, innovation and experimentation, and the fun of sharing group activities together.

What your day will look like:

As part of Clearance, you’ll be on a full-stack DevOps team with other like-minded developers and testers. You’ll collaborate closely to design and code features, you’ll write automated tests but most important of all, you’ll push code into production regularly.

You’ll explore new technologies, learn about software security and experiment as you work on a cutting edge, cloud-based distributed microservice architecture.

Your creativity, troubleshooting and debugging skills will be valuable to maintain cloud infrastructure and ensure a seamless experience for our customers.

About you:

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering or equivalent experience
At least three years experience developing software in the cloud
Passionate about teamwork, state of the art technology, and DevOps
Solid analytical and debugging skills
Good communication and interpersonal skills
Working knowledge of English


Technical Requirements:

A strong foundation in an object-oriented programming language like C#
Knowledge of networking, distributed systems and multi-threaded application development
Knowledge of web technologies such as React, HTML, Javascript, TypeScript and CSS
Knowledge of cloud technologies such as Microsoft Azure


Great if you have:

Experience developing SaaS services in a microservice architecture
Experience with containerization technologies such as Docker and Kubernetes
Practical experience with Visual Studio-GIT
