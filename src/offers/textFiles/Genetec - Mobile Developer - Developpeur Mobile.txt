The team:

We are responsible for the UI (not just buttons and colors) of an enterprise level software system with millions of lines of code and a mobile application used worldwide that is in constant evolution.

So why do you want to work on our team?

Because you’re passionate about serious software development
Because you want to work within a tight knit team of highly talented, highly technical people where your voice will be heard

Because you like to share your knowledge and experience
Because you want to be part of something important

Development of a mobile platform for Android and iOS phones using Xamarin

Put your strong understanding of object-oriented programming to use to design, build and maintain efficient, reusable and reliable code

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering, Software Engineering or Computer Science

Good understanding of SOLID principle and TDD
