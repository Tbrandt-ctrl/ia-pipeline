Job Summary:



Signature Consultants is seeking a Junior Software Engineer for an opportunity with a client located in Austin, TX.







Responsibilities:



Understand and perform basic analysis of previously written software, and be able to modify, develop, and maintain the code
Carry out security, bug-fixing, and app improvement (i.e. detect, investigate, and fix defects)
Participate in design discussions, code walkthroughs, review sessions, and accept constructive feedback; also be happy to review the work of colleagues
Perform complex development tasks with a high level of quality, and within scheduled timeframes
Participate in daily Scrum meetings
Communicates effectively with peers to relay the status of work and raise challenges, concerns, questions, and issues when necessary
Collaborate with the Customer Support and Marketing teams to understand, investigate, and resolve any reported issues and-or implement new ideas



Qualifications:




In-depth and advanced knowledge of HTML and CSS
Intermediate knowledge of SQL
Any web programming framework experience is encouraged to apply (please note we utilize Ruby on Rails)
Minimum of 1 year of development experience on a team (this can include internship experience)
Someone who is willing to learn a new framework and put in the work to help the team at large
TDD knowledge and familiarity
B.S. degree is a preferred qualification, but it is not required

Job Description

We are looking for an enthusiastic, results-driven individual who is easily adaptable and ready to join our team as a Junior Software Developer. The ideal candidate is a self-motivated individual who enjoys learning new things and likes to keep up with new technologies and developments not only for product enhancement but also for personal and professional development. As a software developer, you will work closely with the company's CTO, Development, and Marketing teams, to design, develop and implement software solutions that align with the company's goals including functional and non-functional requirements for new or existing applications.



If you are looking for an exciting new opportunity where your efforts will be appreciated and appropriately compensated, please read on :)



Responsibilities

Understand and perform basic analysis of previously written software, and be able to modify, develop, and maintain the code
